Data-Driven Transport Planning Hackathon

Welcome to the Data-Driven Transport Planning Hackathon!

Presented by the team behind the Propensity to Cycle Tool for Northern Ireland (PCTNI), this afternoon session is an open-ended challenge to develop data analysis, visualisation, or prototype tool ideas that help make transport planning more data-driven.

The Challenge

Develop a tool, visualization, or analysis that addresses a transport planning problem. While we provide datasets from the PCTNI project, your hackathon project does not have to be focused on cycling.

Ideas for inspiration: - High-resolution accessibility mapping. - Visualizing multimodal origin-destination flows. - Predicting the impact of new transport infrastructure. - Identifying gaps in existing data.

External Datasets

Some suggestions for datasets:

Translink Bus Stops - Translink Bus Stop List - Dataset - Open Data NI

Potholes (up to 2025) - Pothole Enquiries - Dataset - Open Data NI

Railway Stations (population with a walk or drive distance) - Northern Ireland Railways Stations - Dataset - Open Data NI

Traffic Count Data (up to 2023 but later is available) Northern Ireland Traffic Count Data - Dataset - Open Data NI

Road Traffic Collision Statistics – collision data can be linked to vehicle and casualty data (available 2013-2024) Police Recorded Injury Road Traffic Collision Statistics Northern Ireland 2024 - Dataset - Open Data NI

Census 2021 origin-destination place of work tables (UK) | Northern Ireland Statistics and Research Agency – for OD flows from 2021 census for NI – available at Census Data Zone.

PCTNI Hackathon Datasets

These datasets are provided as part of the hackathon and can be downloaded from the v0.1.0 Release.

Geography and Demand

  • Zones (SDZ 2021): 2021 Super Data Zones for Northern Ireland (zones_sdz.gpkg).
  • Origin-Destination (OD) Data: Filtered 2021 Census OD data for workplace destinations (od_ni_open_filtered.csv).

Route Networks

  • Fastest Route Network: Network representing the fastest routes (route_network_fastest.gpkg).
  • Quietest Route Network: Network representing the quietest routes (route_network_quietest.gpkg).
  • Processed Cycle Network: A cleaned and processed version of the cycle network (cycle_net_processed.gpkg).

High-Resolution Segments

The following files contain high-resolution route segments, available in both GeoPackage (.gpkg) and R Data (.Rds) formats: - Fastest Segments: Aug 2025, 18 Aug 2025, 19 Aug 2025. - Quietest Segments: Aug 2025, 18 Aug 2025, 19 Aug 2025.

Participation

  • Work individually or in teams.
  • Use any tools or languages (R, Python, JavaScript, etc.).
  • Focus on reproducibility and clear communication.
